Comprehensive Editing

Everything you need for professional manuscript editing, from A.I.-powered analysis to traditional proofreading tools. While A.I.s struggle with creative writing, they can excel at editing when sent clear and precise prompts.

StoryGrind has no subscriptions and no catch. It installs and runs locally on your computer with no cloud storage or subscription required.
Your words are never trapped in some online writing app.

The only out-of-pocket costs are the usage fees for your A.I. API key(s).

Since all of StoryGrind's tools are text-based, your work is portable across any platform or software. All editing software: Word, Pages, Google Docs, Notepad, TextEdit, Vellum-can handle plain text files (.txt) in some way.
